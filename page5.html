Courses and Education Paths

Students can pursue degrees in renewable energy, environmental engineering, and sustainable technologies. Many universities offer specific programs for solar, wind, and biofuel technologies.

After graduation, career options include becoming a solar panel technician, wind turbine engineer, or working in biofuel production and research.
